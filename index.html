Destiny Placement Service (P.) Ltd (DPS) is one of the pioneers, experienced and leading manpower Agencies in Nepal. Registered under the Ministry of Labor. It's main objective notion and views is to be GOOD to others and provide utmost satisfaction, and bettering everybody's destiny by providing them with better opportunities abroad on Employment sector and to solve the unemployment problem of the country besides supporting and boosting the national economy. We can out-right say that DPS has remained one of the most experienced and pioneer manpower agencies (out of 570 agencies) in Nepal. Though established in 1999, DPS has not lagged behind with other manpower companies in quality and superior service and the working mechanism. As one of the leading manpower suppliers, DPS has encompassed and run by a set of experienced executives and staffers who have gathered lot of experience in this field since the last one decade. Till date, DPS has supplied more than 13,715 quality and experienced manpower to multinational companies around the world.

Services

We provide the best overseas consultant solutions

Executive Search & Selection

We help overseas company to supply best manpower according to requirement. We maintain standard code of selection which involves varies process. Only after final selection, they be able to face interview to company representative.

Search & Selection of Manpower for the sponsor

We believe that "Right person at right place in right time" make a big difference. So,we supplies varies skilled, unskilled and semi-skilled manpower according to demand of overseas company.

Permission obtain from Labour Ministry

We do complete legal documentation and take permission from Labour Ministry of each candidate in each step as instructed by Nepal Government.

Obtain visa for worker visa

We do processing of candidates for visa stamping from respective embassy.

Immigration Services

Every candidates needs Labour Department approval for departure. We also provide Immigration Services.

Recruitment & Supply of skilled/Manual labour

We also provide recruitment and supply skilled, unskilled and semiskilled candidates according to demand of country. We conduct several interviews and after selection send CVs for final approval to Overseas Company.
